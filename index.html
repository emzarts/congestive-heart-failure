Symptoms

  • Shortness of breath
  • Fatigue & weakness
  • Swelling of lower legs
  • Weezing and cough

Causes

Predisposition

Congestive Heart Failure is the result of different types of heart failures. People are of higher risk for CHF if they have conditions that affect the flow of blood through their body and the ability for their heart to pump blood. Conditions like these include obesity or diabetes, hypertension, renal failure, and metabolic syndrome (CITE HERE FROM Grassi).

Effect on the Body

Affects the sympathetic nervous system by increasing the amount of adrenaline. Can cause the inner lining of the heart to fail. Can cause the left chamber of the heart to thicken due to the increased stress on its tissues (Figure 1). Also causes the development of atherosclerosis, in which plaque builds up in the arteries that can restrict blood flow.
